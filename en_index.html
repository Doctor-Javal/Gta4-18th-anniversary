GTA IV: 18 Years of Niko Bellic

The Opening: Arrival in Liberty City

Niko Bellic steps off the Platypus with one hope: to leave the shadows of war behind. As the ship docks in Broker, cousin Roman's promises of the "American Dream" echo in the air, but the reality is one of debts and old grudges.

The Law of Liberty City

Surviving in this city means being constantly under the watchful eye of the LCPD. Every street corner can turn into a trap.

"War is where the young and stupid are tricked by the old and bitter into killing each other."

Protagonists & Key Figures

Everything you need to know about the players dominating the streets of Liberty City without spoiling the story.

Niko Bellic

Background: Former soldier and veteran of the Yugoslav Wars.
Affiliation: Independent (Freelance).
Description: He arrives in America to find someone who betrayed his unit years ago. He is a tactical expert, capable of extreme coldness but bound by deep loyalty to family.

Little Jacob

Background: Jamaican arms dealer and key figure in Dukes.
Gang: Jamaican Yardies.
Description: Niko’s closest ally. Jacob is a man of honor in a world of snitches. He manages high-end weapon trades and is the right hand to gang leader Badman.

Patrick "Packie" McReary

Background: The youngest of the McReary crime family from Dukes.
Affiliation: Irish Mob.
Description: An expert, impulsive bank robber. Packie is a pure street soldier who sees Niko as a kindred spirit. He is the key to the city's most daring heists.

Ray Boccino

Background: "Caporegime" for the Pegorino crime family.
Family: Pegorino Family (Alderney).
Description: Slimy and ambitious. Ray operates out of his pizzeria in Alderney. He is the classic mobster who uses Niko as a pawn to climb the ranks of Cosa Nostra.

Dimitri Rascalov

Background: Strategic mastermind of the Russian crime syndicate.
Affiliation: Russian Mafia (Bratva).
Description: A man who believes only in opportunism. Beneath a calm facade, he hides a manipulative nature. He views friendship as a liability.

Is the American Dream an Illusion?

Unlike others, Niko doesn't seek power. His search for Florian Cravic and Darko Brevic leads him to discover that the past cannot be erased. Liberty City is not a land of opportunity, but a labyrinth where every action has a price paid in blood.

SPOILER WARNING: The final choice between Revenge and Deal offers no true victory, only different shades of loss.
