GTA IV: 18 YEARS OF NIKO BELLIC

The Opening: Arrival in Liberty City

Niko Bellic steps off the Platypus with one hope: to leave the shadows of war behind. As the ship docks in Broker, cousin Roman's promises of the "American Dream" echo in the air, but the reality is one of debts and old grudges.

The Law of Liberty City

Surviving in this city means being constantly under the watchful eye of the LCPD. Every street corner can turn into a trap.

"Life is complicated. I killed people, smuggled people, sold people. Maybe here, things will be different."

Character Dossiers

Niko Bellic

Background: Former soldier and Balkan War veteran.

Affiliation: Independent (Freelance).

Description: Cynical and haunted, Niko seeks redemption in America. His personality is defined by fierce family loyalty mixed with cold military expertise. His story is a flight from a past that eventually catches up to him in Liberty City.

Roman Bellic

Background: Niko's cousin and owner of a taxi depot in Broker.

Affiliation: Bellic Enterprises.

Description: An incurable optimist and lover of luxury, Roman drags Niko into trouble due to his gambling debts. Despite his cowardice, his affectionate nature represents Niko's only link to a normal life.

Little Jacob

Background: Jamaican arms dealer based in Dukes.

Affiliation: Yardies.

Description: Loyal and spiritual, Jacob is Niko's closest ally. His calm personality and strong code of honor make him a pillar of stability in a world of traitors.

Dwayne Forge

Background: Old-school North Holland boss, recently released from prison.

Affiliation: North Holland Gang (Former Leader).

Description: Melancholic and disillusioned, Dwayne embodies the failure of old-style crime. His story is marked by depression over his lost empire, finding in Niko a friend who understands the weight of betrayal.

Patrick "Packie" McReary

Background: The youngest and most impulsive of the McReary family.

Affiliation: Irish Mob.

Description: Expert thief and pure street man. Packie is bonded to Niko by a friendship born under fire. His chaotic personality hides deep pain for his family's decline.

Elizabetta Torres

Background: Drug trafficking queen in Bohan for over a decade.

Affiliation: Independent.

Description: Authoritative but worn down by paranoia. Her story shows the collapse of a criminal empire under police pressure, turning her strong character into unstable fury.

Johnny Klebitz

Background: Vice President and operational leader of the Lost MC.

Affiliation: The Lost MC.

Description: Pragmatic and protective of his "brotherhood." Johnny is a biker trying to balance honor and survival in a gang war he didn't seek.

Francis McReary

Background: Corrupt Deputy Police Commissioner.

Affiliation: LCPD.

Description: Hypocritical and manipulative. He uses Niko to clean up his criminal past in exchange for silence, representing the moral corruption of Liberty City's institutions.

Gerald "Gerry" McReary

Background: The criminal mastermind of the McRearys.

Affiliation: Irish Mob.

Description: Cold and calculating, Gerry plans the family's moves even from prison. He trusts no one and sees the world only through the lens of power and control.

Derrick McReary

Background: Former IRA bomber with a past of exile.

Affiliation: Irish Mob (Inactive).

Description: Broken by guilt and addiction, Derrick is the shadow of a man. His fragile personality makes him the weakest and most tragic link of the McReary family.

United Liberty Paper Man

Background: Undercover government agent.

Affiliation: United States Government.

Description: Sophisticated, mysterious, and ruthless. He treats Niko as a disposable asset, showing that true power in Liberty City lies in government offices, not on the streets.

Vlad Glebov

Background: Low-level Russian loan shark.

Affiliation: Russian Mob.

Description: Bullying and arrogant, Vlad makes the fatal mistake of underestimating Niko. His death triggers the war with the major Russian families.

Dimitri Rascalov

Background: Russian criminal mastermind and primary antagonist.

Affiliation: Russian Mob.

Description: A pure manipulator. Dimitri doesn't believe in honor, only opportunism. He is the dark reflection of the betrayal Niko has tried to escape his entire life.
